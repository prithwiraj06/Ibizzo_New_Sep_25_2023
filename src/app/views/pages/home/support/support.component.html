Registration

Registration is a Free and simple process of getting you onboard with us.

You can get registered with us by following these simple steps:

Step 1 : Visit iBizzo.com and click on Register button.
Step 2 :Enter your basic details such as your name, company name, Ph no and Email. Click Continue..
Step 3: Now enter a few more details about your company (your business category, product, pincode, partner organization).
Step 4: Create your password and log in to your Dashboard
